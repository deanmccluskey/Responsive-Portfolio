About Me






Welcome to my Portfolio Site! First, a little bit about me...

"WordSmith, Imagist and CodePro!!!"

I am a Software Developer (in training), who holds a Bachelor's degree in Journalism/ Photojournalism from San Jose State University. Two weeks out of school, I was working as a photographer for the Tahoe Daily Tribune! But I found the salary less than desirable, so I returned to the hospitality industry which had put me through college.

Years later, I returned to school to study Computer Science at Diablo Valley College. I took courses from basic coding to advanced programming concepts using Assembly, C/C++, and Java/ JavaScript. While I worked shortly in the technical field in the positions of Help Desk, Customer Support and Software Quality Assurance, I never made the transition to Developer. Since then, I have continued work as a waiter, a bartender, and even spent some time as a casino dealer, learning every game in the house!!!

But now the time has come for me to return to my passion! While I loved my photography, I love coding more!!! I think the role of software developer, as that of a 21st century scribe, connecting their clients to the masses. And the practice of development, especially app functionality, is somewhat akin to a mental "Chinese puzzle" (something I loved as a kid)!

So here I AM, part poet, part artist, and full-on technophile! And here is my work!!!
